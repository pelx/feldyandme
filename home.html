The Feldenkrais Method

A healthy person is one who can live fully his unavowed dreams...

Moshe Feldenkrais

Much has been written about Moshe Feldenkrais and the principles that he integrated as both a scientist and as a black belt martial arts expert into the Feldenkrais method. It is ones own development that comes to the fore in relation to his principles. The discovery that sometimes doing things in a simple and easy manner can be far more beneficial than exerting effort.

Elusive Obvious

However what has fascinated me in this work is that simple really is the Elusive Obvious. The harder that I tried the less I was able to understand. Letting go of our goal orientated principles is hard and yet when accepted the easier and lighter that I felt within myself and the more that my movements became less rigid and flowed with ease.
